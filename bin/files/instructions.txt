Welcome to the Royal Game of Ur.

This is a simple application to facilitate the playing of the Royal Game of Ur.

Instructions:
Players take turns rolling the dice and moving their pieces.
After your oll the dice you may move any of your pieces along your path according to the number you rolled. (The application will highlight any legal moves.)
An exact roll is need to move off the board.
You cannot land on a square if you already have a piece there.
If you land on a square with an opponents piece the opposing piece is sent home.
The first player to move all of their pieces off the end of the board is the winner.
If you land on a tile marked with an R you take an extra turn.
If you roll a 0 you lose your turn.
